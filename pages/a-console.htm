Management Console

Category: Knowledge Management, Internal, CRUD
Design Duration: 3 months
Version: 1.00 (2019)
Pages: 6
Status: Built

The Management Console provided the data administrators an easy and consolidated way to carry out tasks beyond what the Knowledgement Management applications could do such as maintaining list of deleted profiles besides others. The console design had a familiar layout with easy, discoverable and focussed task controls.

What and why?

Data is valuable. Quality data curation for timely responses to business development opportunities is the main goal of Knowledge Management tools.
